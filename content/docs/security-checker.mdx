## What this tool does

FORGE's security checker displays the on-chain risk signals for any token: authority status, holder concentration, and liquidity lock status. It is designed to give you the same picture that professional DEX scanners show — without leaving FORGE.

## Understanding the indicators

### Authority badges

| Badge | Color | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Mint Revoked | Green ✅ | Supply is permanently capped |
| Mint Active | Orange ⚠️ | Supply can increase at any time |
| Freeze Revoked | Green ✅ | No wallet can be frozen |
| Freeze Active | Orange ⚠️ | Wallets can be frozen by authority holder |
| Immutable | Green ✅ | Metadata cannot change |
| Mutable | Orange ⚠️ | Metadata can change |

**A token with all three authorities revoked (or showing all green) has the cleanest possible authority profile.** This does not make it a good investment — it only means these specific risks are absent.

### Holder concentration

The concentration gauge shows the percentage of supply held by the top 10 wallets.

| Range | Indicator |
|-------|-----------|
| Under 20% | Low concentration |
| 20–50% | Moderate |
| Over 50% | High — consider implications |
| Over 80% | Very high — significant price impact risk |

Concentration is a structural metric. High concentration is not inherently bad (many legitimate projects have early large holders), but it does mean a few wallets can significantly move the price.

### Liquidity status

Shows whether the token has active liquidity pools and, if so, whether LP tokens are burned or held by a wallet:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| LP Burned | Liquidity is locked permanently — cannot be removed |
| LP Held | Liquidity can be removed by the holder at any time |
| No Liquidity | No DEX pool detected |

### What FORGE does not check

- Project legitimacy or team background
- Smart contract audits (for Token-2022 extensions)
- External promises or roadmap credibility
- Social media or website authenticity

For deeper analysis, see external tools like Rugcheck.xyz, RugDoc, or Birdeye's security tab.

<Warning>
**Security indicators are informational only.** They reflect on-chain state at a point in time. FORGE does not endorse or rate projects. Never invest based solely on on-chain metrics.
</Warning>

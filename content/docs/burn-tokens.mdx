## What this tool does

Solana Token Burn lets you send tokens to the Solana burn address, permanently removing them from circulating supply. This is an on-chain operation — the tokens cease to exist.

## What it costs

Burning tokens is **free** on Solana Token. You pay only Solana network gas, which is typically less than 0.001 SOL.

## Before you start

- Tokens you want to burn in your wallet
- Your wallet connected to Solana Token
- Certainty — burning is irreversible

<Warning>
**Burning is permanent and irreversible.** There is no undo. Once tokens are burned, they cannot be recovered. Confirm the amount before signing.
</Warning>

## Step-by-step

### Step 1 — Go to Solana Token → Burn

Navigate to the Burn tool from the main navigation.

### Step 2 — Select token

Choose the token you want to burn from the dropdown. Solana Token shows your current balance for that token.

### Step 3 — Enter amount

Type the amount to burn, or click **Max** to burn your entire balance. The preview shows the remaining supply after the burn.

### Step 4 — Confirm and sign

Check the confirmation box acknowledging the action is irreversible. Click **Burn tokens** and approve the transaction in your wallet.

## What happens on-chain

The transaction calls the SPL Token program's `burn` instruction, which reduces the mint's total supply counter and destroys the tokens from your account. The reduction is immediately visible on Solscan and all explorers.

<Warning>
**You will burn the exact amount shown.** Double-check the number before confirming — common mistakes include wrong decimal placement (burning 1,000,000 tokens instead of 1 due to a decimal error).
</Warning>

## Burning LP tokens

Burning LP (liquidity pool) tokens is a separate action that signals locked liquidity — it means the liquidity cannot be removed, which reduces rug pull risk. Use **Solana Token Tools → Burn LP** for this. It works the same way but operates on LP mint addresses instead of your token.
